Sekhan theke ekta truck e chepe Vizhay, Norther Siberiar ek prottonto settlement. 27e January suru holo Vizahay theke Otorten mountain range er dike podobroje jatra. Porer din Yudin er tibro paye byatha suru hoy. Karon : Sciatic nerve pain. Yudin fire ase. ekta sustho jibon katiye 2013 y mara jan Yuri Yudin, Dyatlov pass team er ekmatro survivor.

Edike 1 tarikh sokale baki 9 jon er jatra suru korte korte ektu derii hoye gelo. 4km hatar pore labaz er jongol e camp khatiye barti jinis potro ebong khabar rakhte rakhte bajlo bikel 5 ta. camp fela holo Kholat Syakhl er dhal e. Kholat Syakhl porbot er namkoron okhankar ancholic manshi bhasay. Tar ingreji torjoma korle daray "Dead Mountain". (Seriously?)

6ta theke 7tar modhhe sobar dinner kora hole, sombhoboto dujon camp er baire jay kono kaj e. Erpore ki hoy tar hodish ar kono documents ei nei.
